Accuro pro offers a variety of coverage options to best serve your practice needs

Coverage Options

Claims-Made Coverage

Provides coverage for incidents that occur after the retroactive date and which are reported to Accuro pro during your active policy, or any extended reporting period.

Occurrence Coverage

Provides coverage for incidents that occur during your policy period irrespective of when the claim is reported.

Modified Claims-Made Coverage (Not available in New York)

Provides coverage on a claims-made basis with an automatic prepaid extended reporting coverage (Tail). Also known as Occurrence Plus.

New York State Excess Liability Coverage (Section 18)

An additional $1,000,000 per claim / $3,000,000 annual aggregate limit of coverage excess of your primary limit is available at no cost to qualifying eligible physicians through New York State.

Direct Pay Excess Coverage (*Only available in New York)

This additional layer of coverage of $1,000,000 per claim / $3,000,000 annual aggregate is available to physicians who do not maintain or qualify for NY Section 18 Excess coverage and wish to purchase an excess policy. *Increased primary limits of liability are available to policyholders in Connecticut, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey.

Medical Entity Coverage for your PC, LLC, PLLC, or Partnership

Coverage is available to insure your entity, its members in their capacity as such, and non-physician employees of the entity for their respective acts and omissions. It also covers the entity and its members for their vicarious liability arising from the alleged acts and omissions of others.

Administrative Action Legal Representation Coverage

At no additional cost, all Accuro pro medical professional liability policies include up to $25,000 in coverage for fees related to legal representation, subject to certain limitations, in connection with administrative disciplinary actions.
